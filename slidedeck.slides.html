Analyzing Ford GoBike System Data¶

Introduction¶

Ford GoBike is a public bicycle-sharing system in the San Francisco Bay Area of California. Currently, the Ford GoBike network has over 2,600 bicycles in 262 stations across San Francisco, East Bay and San Jose, beginning service in August 2013 as a Bay Area Bike Share. The system was officially launched on 28 June 2017 as Ford GoBike in conjunction with Ford Motor Company.
